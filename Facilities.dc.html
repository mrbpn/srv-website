Facilities

Everything a school day needs, on one campus.

From getting to school, to a healthy lunch, to a quiet place to rest — the day-to-day essentials are taken care of.

01

Transportation

Transportation facilities are available for all our students, covering the routes around Madhyapur Thimi and neighbouring areas, so getting to and from school is one less thing to worry about.

02

Lunch & snacks

We provide a healthy and nutritious diet for our students, with balanced meals and snacks planned to support growing children through a full school day.

03

Refreshment & nap room

A cozy nap room is provided for our youngest students, giving pre-primary children a calm space to rest and recharge during the day.

04

Hostel

Hostel accommodation is available for students who need to stay closer to school, with supervised care outside class hours.

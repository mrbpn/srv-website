About the school

A caring school with high expectations.

Sahara Rastriya Vidhyalaya is a school in Kaushaltar, Madhyapur Thimi, offering a broad and balanced curriculum from pre-primary (PG) through Grade 8. We meet all our students' needs and interests while adhering to statutory requirements — with interactive teaching technology and participatory learning in every classroom.

The pride of the school is its pupils.

We aim to create a happy and caring atmosphere where consideration and respect are shown by pupils and adults alike. With a commitment to shaping its pupils for the future, SRV offers an exciting, stimulating and rich curriculum, built on objective reasons for learning.

Mission

To provide a welcoming, exciting and caring environment where all students can be happy, confident and motivated; to encourage our students to take pride in themselves, their school and their nation; and to help them develop critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, communication, leadership, technology and media literacy, initiative and social skills — the skills that keep pace with today's modern world and grow good, competing citizens.

Vision

Sahara Rastriya Vidhyalaya was opened to meet the highly complex and competitive needs of the 21st century. Our programmes — both academic and co-curricular — have been designed to address those needs, preparing every student not only for examinations but for the world beyond them.

What we stand for
Admire

Respect for oneself, for others and for learning is the foundation of everything we do.

Approach

Curiosity and effort — we teach students to approach problems with method and confidence.

Achieve

Results that follow naturally from good habits, strong teaching and genuine care.
